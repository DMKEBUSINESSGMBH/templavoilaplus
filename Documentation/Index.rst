----

TemplaVoilà! Plus is an alternate TYPO3 Page Modul and content handler. It gives
you the possibility for pages and content elements to create own backend layouts,
enhanced with individual fields, create containers and much more. In frontend
your content can be generated by different renderer like the build in XPath.
Your templates can will be organized in extensions for full deployability.
